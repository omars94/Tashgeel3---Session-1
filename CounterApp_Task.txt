ReactJS Task: Counter App

Objective:
    Create a simple ReactJS application that includes:
        A number displayed on screen (the counter).
        Three buttons:
            + to increment the count.
            - to decrement the count.
            Reset to reset the count to 0.
    Bonus: 
        - A toggle button to switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode.
        - Disable the - button when the count is at 0.
